Our Mission

Impact Cercle aims to create a dynamic environment for students to develop leadership, creativity, and teamwork.

We focus on building impactful projects that benefit both our community and student life at ISET Kelibia.

Our Vision

We aspire to become a leading student organization recognized for innovation, collaboration, and positive change.

Through events, workshops, and community projects, we empower students to make a real difference.

Achievements

  • Organized multiple social and environmental awareness campaigns
  • Collaborated with local NGOs and institutions for impactful projects
  • Hosted student innovation and entrepreneurship events
